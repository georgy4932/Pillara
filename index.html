The challenge

Staying consistent
isn’t as simple as remembering.

Missed doses often happen because life gets busy, routines change, instructions feel unclear, or support is scattered. Pillara is being built to reduce that friction.

01

Remembering routines can be difficult when there is more than one medicine involved.

02

Medicine information can feel confusing or hard to interpret without plain-language support.

03

Over-the-counter products can raise questions that are worth discussing with a pharmacist.

04

Appointments can feel rushed, and it is easy to forget the questions you meant to ask.

05

Carers often carry the extra responsibility of keeping someone else’s routine organised.

What the app does

Built around consistency,
not just tracking.

⏰

Medication routine reminders

Set a schedule for each medicine and receive helpful reminders so staying consistent feels easier and less stressful.

📖

Clearer medicine information

Keep medicine details in one place with plain-language information that helps you feel more informed when speaking with your GP or pharmacist.

Appointment preparation

Questions & answers

Things people
want to know.

Is Pillara a replacement for my doctor or pharmacist?
No. Pillara is a support tool that helps you stay organised and informed between appointments. Always consult your GP or pharmacist for clinical decisions.
Which devices will Pillara work on?
Pillara is planned for iPhone, iPad, Android devices, and web. Your platform preference helps us prioritise.
Can it help if I take several medicines?
Pillara is being designed to support people managing multiple medicines by helping organise reminders, notes, and appointment questions in one place.
How do OTC information prompts work?
Pillara is being designed to help highlight information that may be worth checking with a pharmacist when considering over-the-counter products.
Is Pillara suitable for carers?
Yes. Pillara is being designed to support carers and families managing medicine routines on behalf of loved ones.
Is my health information secure?
Pillara is being built with privacy and responsible data handling in mind. Full privacy details will be published before launch.
When will the app be available?
Pillara is currently in development. Join the waitlist to be notified when early access opens.
Can pharmacies or GP practices get involved?
Yes. Healthcare organisations interested in pilots or partnerships are welcome to join the waitlist or contact us directly.
